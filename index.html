Contact: noa@jhu.edu

I'm a research scientist at the JHU HLTCOE interested in natural language processing and machine learning. I also have an appointment in the Department of Computer Science and work closely with PhD students and faculty at the Center for Language and Speech Processing.

PhD students: David Mueller (with Mark Dredze) and Aleem Khan (with Benjamin Van Durme).

About me: I obtained my PhD from JHU in 2015, advised by Jason Eisner and Mark Dredze. Before that, I worked at BBN Technologies. Fun facts: I grew up in England and Switzerland, speak French, and I'm an avid hiker and cyclist.
